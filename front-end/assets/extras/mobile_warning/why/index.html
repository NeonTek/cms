Why Neonteks's CMS Flexbox Editor is Temporarily Unavailable on Mobile Devices

At Neontek, we pride ourselves on providing a seamless and user-friendly experience for everyone. However, you may have noticed that the CMS Flexbox is currently unavailable when accessed through a mobile phone. We want to take a moment to explain why this decision as made and what steps we are taking to resolve this issue.

Understanding the Issue

Our website is powered by GrapesJS to enable smooth editing and customisation of web pages. This system is integral to how we manage and provide value to the our users.
Unfortunately, we recently encountered a technical challenge with the drag-and-drop functionality in our CMS. Specifically, when accessed through a mobile device, this feature fails to perform as expected.

Why Disable Mobile Access

You might wonder why we decided to disable mobile access instead of leaving the site operational. Here's why:

  • User Experience Matters: A partially functional website can be frustrating, especially when users expect a polished experience.
  • Maintaining Professionalism: Rather than delivering a subpar or buggy experience, we believe its better to pause access until we can confidently restore full functionality.
  • Efficient Problem Solving: Limiting mobile access allows our team to focus on addressing the issue without worrying about interactions causing further complications.

What We're Doing to Fix It

Rest assured, this is only a temporary measure. Our development team is actively working to resolve the drag-and-drop issue and restore full mobile functionality. Here's what we're doing:

  • Collaborating with CMS Experts: We're in touch with the CMS developers to troubleshoot the problem at its root.
  • Testing Solutions: Our team is running extensive tests on both desktop and mobile devices to ensure the fix works seamlessly across all platforms.
  • Exploring Alternatives: In case the current CMS limitations persist, we're researching alternative tools or plugins to enhance mobile compatibility.

When Will Mobile Access Return?

We understand that this may inconvenience some users, and we sincerely apologize for any disruption caused. While we can't provide an exact timeline, we are prioritizing this fix and will update you as soon as the mobile experience is back to normal.

A Message to Our Users

We value your patience and understanding as we work through this challenge. Our commitment to quality and user satisfaction drives every decision we make, and we're confident that the steps we're taking now will result in a better, more reliable experience for everyone in the long run.

In the meantime, we encourage you to access our website via a desktop or laptop computer for an uninterrupted experience. Should you have any questions or need assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at support@neontek.co.ke.

Thank you for standing by us. Together, we'll overcome this temporary hurdle and continue to deliver the excellent online experience you've come to expect from us.

Sincerely,
The Neontek Team.
